Core, Proven Learning Theories

  • Spaced Repetition → Review material at increasing intervals to fight the forgetting curve.
  • Active Recall → Test yourself by retrieving information without looking at notes.
  • Interleaving Practice → Mix different topics or problem types in one session to improve adaptability.
  • Practice Testing → Simulate exam conditions to strengthen retrieval and reduce anxiety.
  • Elaboration → Explain concepts in your own words and connect them to prior knowledge.
  • Metacognition → Reflect on what you know vs. don’t know to guide study time efficiently.
